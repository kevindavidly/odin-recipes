Chicken Ramen

A dish that take 30 minutes to make with rich flavors.

Ingredients

  • Nori
  • Shiitake
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Spring Onion
  • Chicken Breast
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Egg Noodle
  • Oil
  • Chicken Stock

Steps

  1. Preheat oven at 400 F using upper and lower heat to boil water in two pots
  2. Add everything in a point, cover and simmer for 20 minutes
  3. Chicken:Char the chicken on a hot iron skillet for 1-2 minutes, on both sides. Then place in the oven on the upper â…“ rack, for 10-15 minutes or until fully cooked.
  4. Boil eggs for six minutes then place in cool water after for easy peeling
  5. Use other pot for noodles and follow instructions on how you want your noodles
  6. Discard garlic and ginger from the broth. Slice the shiitake and some spring onion. Slice the chicken in thin slices.
  7. Ladle some broth into 2 bowls. Add in the noodles, followed by chicken slices and shiitake. Slice the eggs and place in each bowl. Finish off with some sliced spring onion and tuck in a piece of nori sheet on one side of the bowl. Serve immediately!
